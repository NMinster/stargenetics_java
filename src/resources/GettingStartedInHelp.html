Getting started with StarGenetics

Mating Experiments
* Select a male and a female organism from the 'Strains' box and drag them to the 'Mating site' box.
* Click the 'Mate' button.
* Progeny data will appear in summary form under the 'Summary' tab or in individual form under the 'Individual' tab.

Performing Yeast Experiments
* To test strain(s) in different media or perform a complementation test between strain(s), click on the 'Non-tetrad experiment' button.
* To perform a tetrad analysis, including mating type testing for tetrads, click on the 'Tetrad experiment' button.

Saving Experiments
* To save data generated during experiments, click on 'File->Save'.
   You can open the saved StarGenetics file later to continue with your experiments.
